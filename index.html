Introduction

In Call of Duty Zombies, Easter eggs are hidden secrets that players can unlock through specific actions. These Easter eggs often include unique challenges, hidden cutscenes, and special rewards.

Black Ops 3

Shadows of Evil

The *Shadows of Evil* Easter egg is an elaborate quest requiring teamwork and precise execution. Below are the detailed steps to complete it:

  1. Turn on the Power: Activate the Beast Mode in each district to unlock key items and power up machines. Use the Beast to zap power boxes and break chains to open areas.
  2. Collect the Summoning Key: Retrieve the Summoning Key from the spawn area after activating Beast Mode for the first time.
  3. Perform Rituals:
    • Retrieve the necessary items for each ritual (e.g., Lawyer’s Pen, Detective’s Badge, Boxer’s Belt, and Femme Fatale’s Wig).
    • Perform rituals in the Junction, Canal District, Footlight District, and Waterfront District.
  4. Open Pack-a-Punch: After completing all four rituals, head to the Rift and place the Gateworms on the pedestals to unlock Pack-a-Punch.
  5. Upgrade the Swords:
    • Acquire the Apothicon Sword by collecting three symbols using the train and opening the corresponding doors.
    • Complete challenges given by the ghost figure to upgrade the sword.
  6. Summon the Shadow Man: After upgrading the swords, activate the rituals in the Rift to summon the Shadow Man. Use powerful weapons to defeat him.
  7. Complete the Final Battle: Work together to defeat the Apothicon Overlord by directing the energy beams and charging the Gateworms. This is the final step to unlock the Easter egg cutscene.

Der Eisendrache

The *Der Eisendrache* Easter egg, *My Brother’s Keeper*, involves defeating a Keeper and saving the world. Here's how to complete it:

  1. Build the Wrath of the Ancients Bow: Feed the three dragon statues around the map by killing zombies near them.
  2. Upgrade the Bow: Choose and complete one of the four elemental bow upgrade quests: Fire, Lightning, Wolf, or Void.
  3. Activate the Teleporter: Collect the three fuses and power up the Death Ray to access the teleporter.
  4. Build the Keeper: Collect the parts to construct the Keeper Protector at the pyramid in the Undercroft.
  5. Place the Gatekeeper: Position the Keeper at the designated spots to start the boss fight.
  6. Defeat the Keeper: Work as a team to defeat the Keeper boss by attacking the weak spots and avoiding its attacks.
  7. Enjoy the Cutscene: After the boss is defeated, enjoy the ending cinematic and rewards.

Zetsubou no Shima

The *Zetsubou no Shima* Easter egg requires completing a series of tasks to defeat the giant spider boss and free Takeo. Here's how to do it:

  1. Turn on the Power: Activate power in both generator rooms and then lower the cage to trap the zombie, electrifying the bunker.
  2. Complete Skull of Nan Sapwe: Retrieve and cleanse the four skulls to unlock this powerful specialist weapon.
  3. Build the KT-4 Wonder Weapon: Collect the spider venom, green liquid from the lab, and plant samples to craft the KT-4.
  4. Upgrade the KT-4: Gather additional materials (e.g., Rainbow Water and plant samples) to craft the Masamune, the KT-4's upgraded version.
  5. Defeat the Giant Spider: Head into the spider lair and kill the giant spider boss to obtain its venom.
  6. Release Takeo: Use the Masamune and Skull of Nan Sapwe to progress to the final sequence and free Takeo.
  7. Complete the Boss Fight: Defeat the Thrashers and complete the final steps to finish the Easter egg and view the ending cutscene.

Gorod Krovi

The *Gorod Krovi* Easter egg involves battling a dragon and Nikolai 1.0. Here's how to complete it:

  1. Build the Dragon Network Controller: Collect the parts by completing trials and use it to summon the dragon.
  2. Enter the Pack-a-Punch Area: Ride the dragon to the Pack-a-Punch room and retrieve key items.
  3. Complete the Gauntlet of Siegfried: Finish challenges to unlock the Gauntlet of Siegfried specialist weapon.
  4. Destroy the Dragon: Work together to bring down the massive dragon boss.
  5. Defeat Nikolai 1.0: Engage in a final boss fight with Nikolai’s mech suit and complete the Easter egg.

Revelations

The *Revelations* Easter egg is the ultimate quest in Black Ops 3, tying together the Zombies storyline. Here's how to complete it:

  1. Activate the Corruption Engines: Power up all four Corruption Engines around the map to open the Pack-a-Punch area.
  2. Collect the Apothicon Servant and Upgrade: Craft and upgrade the Apothicon Servant using key parts found in the map.
  3. Activate the Runes: Solve the symbols puzzle on the Keeper Protector’s altar to activate the Runes of Creation.
  4. Fight the Shadow Man: Engage in an epic boss fight with the Shadow Man, using the Runes and upgraded weapons.
  5. Complete the Final Step: Charge the Summoning Key and throw it into Sophia to finish the Easter egg.

Black Ops 2

Buried - Mined Games

The *Buried* Easter egg, *Mined Games*, requires teamwork and choosing between Richtofen’s or Maxis’s side. Steps are similar but diverge toward the end:

  1. Power Up the Map: Turn on the power and unlock areas with Leroy’s help.
  2. Build the Guillotine (Richtofen) or Hangman’s Gallows (Maxis): Collect the necessary parts scattered around the map.
  3. Charge the Orbs: Shoot glowing orbs with the Paralyzer or Galvaknuckles to activate them.
  4. Complete the Switch Puzzle: Work together to complete the maze-like puzzle involving lights and switches.
  5. Final Step: Depending on the side you chose, either help Richtofen control the Aether or Maxis destroy it. Completing this unlocks the cutscene.

Origins - Little Lost Girl

The *Origins* Easter egg involves freeing Samantha from Agartha. It's a challenging quest with many steps:

  1. Activate Generators: Power up all six generators on the map.
  2. Build the Elemental Staffs: Collect parts for the Fire, Ice, Lightning, and Wind Staffs by solving puzzles and defeating enemies.
  3. Upgrade the Staffs: Complete specific challenges for each staff in Agartha to upgrade them.
  4. Destroy the Seal: Use the upgraded staffs to destroy the seal in the Excavation Site.
  5. Free Samantha: Place the staffs in their designated spots and complete the final challenges to open Agartha and release Samantha.

Mob of the Dead - Pop Goes the Weasel

The *Mob of the Dead* Easter egg tells the tragic story of Alcatraz inmates. Here’s how to complete it:

  1. Build the Plane: Collect five parts scattered around the map to construct the escape plane.
  2. Fly to the Golden Gate Bridge: Use the plane to fly to the bridge and Pack-a-Punch your weapons.
  3. Complete the Cycle: Enter Afterlife mode and shock the generators on the bridge to continue the cycle.
  4. Break the Cycle: In the final step, either break the cycle by killing Weasel or continue the cycle by defeating the other players.

Die Rise - High Maintenance

The *Die Rise* Easter egg, *High Maintenance*, requires choosing between Maxis or Richtofen's side. Here's a simplified version of the steps:

  1. Activate the Dragon Symbols: Stand on the pressure plates to light up the dragon symbols.
  2. Build the Navcard Table: Gather the parts and assemble the table.
  3. Align the Tower: Complete challenges involving the elevators and floor symbols to power the tower.
  4. Complete the Final Step: Follow either Richtofen’s or Maxis’s instructions to finish the Easter egg.

Tranzit - Tower of Babble

The *Tranzit* Easter egg, *Tower of Babble*, involves choosing Richtofen or Maxis’s side and powering the Obelisk Tower:

  1. Turn on the Power: Activate the power station.
  2. Obelisk Table: Build the Navcard Reader at the Obelisk Tower.
  3. Charge the Tower: Use either EMP grenades (Richtofen) or a turbine (Maxis) to power the Obelisk.
  4. Complete the Easter Egg: Finish the specific tasks for your chosen side to power the tower and complete the Easter egg.
